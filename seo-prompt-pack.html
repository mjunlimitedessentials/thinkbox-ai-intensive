What is SEO & Why AI Changes Everything

"Explain SEO to me like I'm a small business owner who has never studied marketing. Then give me 5 reasons why my [type of business] needs SEO right now."

Module 02

Keyword Research — Find What Your Audience is Actually Searching

Keywords are the foundation of SEO. This module teaches you how to find the exact words and phrases your ideal clients type into Google — and use AI to do all the research in seconds.

✦ Power Prompt
"Act as an SEO expert. Give me 20 long-tail keywords for a [your business type] located in [your city/region]. Include keywords for local search, problem-based search, and service-based search. Format as a table."
✦ Bonus Prompt
"From this keyword list, identify the top 5 that have high buyer intent and low competition. Explain why each one was chosen."
💡 Why this works: Long-tail keywords are easier to rank for AND attract people who are ready to buy — not just browse.
Module 03

SEO Content Creation — Write Blogs & Pages That Rank

Content is KING in SEO. Google rewards websites that consistently publish helpful, keyword-rich content. This module shows you how to use AI to write blog posts and web pages that Google loves.

✦ Blog Post Prompt
"Write a 600-word SEO blog post for my [type of business] targeting the keyword '[your keyword]'. Include an engaging intro, 3 subheadings with H2 tags, a call to action at the end, and naturally place the keyword 4-6 times. Tone: professional but conversational."
✦ Web Page Prompt
"Write SEO-optimized copy for my [Services/About/Home] page. My business is [description]. My target audience is [audience]. Primary keyword: [keyword]. Include a hero headline, 3 benefit sections, and a closing CTA."
💡 Why this works: Google doesn't just rank websites — it ranks pages. Each page you publish is another chance to appear in search results.
Module 04

Meta Descriptions & Title Tags — The Click Magnet Formula

Meta descriptions and title tags are what people SEE on Google before they click. Most business owners ignore them — which is a huge missed opportunity. This module makes writing them effortless.

✦ Meta Prompt
"Write 3 versions of a meta title and meta description for a webpage about [topic/service]. Target keyword: [keyword]. Keep the title under 60 characters and the description under 155 characters. Make each version feel compelling and click-worthy."
💡 Why this works: A great meta description can double your click-through rate — meaning more traffic without ranking any higher.
Module 05

Google Business Profile — Dominate Local Search

If you serve local clients — this module is GOLD. Your Google Business Profile is the #1 way to show up when someone searches for your service nearby. AI helps you write every section perfectly.

✦ Google Business Prompt
"Write an optimized Google Business Profile description for my [type of business] in [city, state]. Include my primary service keywords, mention what makes me unique, and end with a call to action. Keep it under 750 characters."
✦ Google Post Prompt
"Write 5 Google Business Posts for my [business type] promoting [service/offer/event]. Each post should be 150-200 words, include a keyword naturally, and end with a CTA button label (Call Now, Learn More, Book Online, etc)."
💡 Why this works: Google Business Profiles appear BEFORE regular search results for local searches. This is free real estate you can't afford to ignore.
Module 06

Social Media SEO — How to Get Found on Instagram, YouTube & Pinterest

SEO isn't just for Google anymore. Instagram, YouTube, and Pinterest are all search engines. This module teaches you how to optimize your social media content so it shows up everywhere your audience is looking.

✦ Instagram SEO Prompt
"Write an SEO-optimized Instagram bio for my [business type]. Include my primary keyword naturally, what I do, who I serve, and a CTA. Keep it under 150 characters."
✦ YouTube SEO Prompt
"Write an SEO-optimized YouTube video title, description, and 15 tags for a video about [topic]. Target keyword: [keyword]. Description should be 200 words and include the keyword in the first sentence."
✦ Caption SEO Prompt
"Write 5 Instagram captions for [topic/offer] that naturally include the keyword [keyword]. Each caption should be engaging, end with a question or CTA, and include 10 relevant hashtags."
💡 Why this works: Appearing across multiple platforms multiplies your visibility. One piece of content — optimized right — can bring in leads from Google, Instagram, AND YouTube simultaneously.
